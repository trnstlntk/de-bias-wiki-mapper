DE-BIAS is a project supported by Europeana’s DE-BIAS Vocabulary to promote inclusive and respectful language in cultural heritage and Wikimedia content. It provides a controlled list of outdated or potentially offensive terms (around 700 terms across multiple languages) so users can understand why they’re problematic and find better alternatives.
