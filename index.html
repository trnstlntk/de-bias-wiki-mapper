Europeana’s DE-BIAS Vocabulary has been developed to promote inclusive and respectful language in cultural heritage. It provides a controlled list of outdated or potentially offensive terms (around 700 terms across multiple languages) so users can understand why they’re problematic and find better alternatives.

Content Warning: The table below contains historical and contemporary terms that may be offensive or derogatory. They are presented for reference and analysis only— to help identify bias and improve content—and not to endorse their use.

This specific tool, the DE-BIAS Wiki Mapper, is built with and upon the DE-BIAS vocabulary resources. It provides an interface that helps editors of Wikimedia projects explore the terms with clarity and ease, and provides direct tools and connections to analyze and improve the usage of these problematic terms in Wikimedia projects - mainly Wikidata and Wikipedias in the languages in which the DE-BIAS vocabulary is also available.
